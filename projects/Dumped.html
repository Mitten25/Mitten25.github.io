Dumped

Extreme Space Based Garbage Collection

Primary Roles: Game Design, Backend Engineer, UI/Front End Engineer, and Sound


Year: Spring 2017


Platforms: MS Windows, MAC OS


Tools and Technologies: Unity, Adobe Illustrator, Maya


Description: A creative take on the challenges of garbage collection. This space based time trial game has players collect every garbage can from a planet in the shortest amount of time. Timing your truck's claws is the key to success. Travel to different worlds and encounter new challenges along the way. Explore planets as you zip around them as quickly as possible. Customize your truck as you sport your garbage collection skills. A solar system of trash-ridden planets awaits!

Dumped was my first true 3D game and it was something. Early design was all about travel around a planet in a Mario Galaxy type way. This took a while to refine, but was done very effectively. Realistic truck physics followed soon after. Having gravity behave properly on a small planet had us develop outside of the Unity engine. The game's environment is fully destructible, which gave us many lessons in rendering and collisions. 3D Animation was a new challenge that required a large amount of trial and error. In the end, Dumped has the base to progress to a publishable level.
